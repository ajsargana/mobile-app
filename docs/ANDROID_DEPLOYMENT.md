# AURA50 Android Deployment Guide

Complete guide to deploy AURA50 Android app to Google Play Store.

## Quick Steps

1. **Google Play Console** ($25 one-time) - play.google.com/console
2. **Create Package**: com.aura50.wallet
3. **Generate Signing Key**: `keytool -genkeypair`
4. **Build with EAS**: `eas build --platform android`
5. **Upload to Play Console**
6. **Submit for Review**
